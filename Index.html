         BUSINESS HISTORY

Curio hub is a technology initiative founded in 2023 by Ms Shongile Makhubele and Praise Mdaka. The organisation was created because of the lack of digital literacy and technology opportunities in the communities.
It began as a small business that focuses on helping individuals understand basic digital tools and it developed into a hub that encourages innovation and technology learning. Curio Hub aims to empower individuals by providing digital knowledge, mentorship and resources. It supports people who want to explore the tech opportunities in tech industry and helps them transform ideas into practical digital solutions.
